ABOUT

IEEE SB NSSCE

IEEE Student branch chapter of NSS College of engineering, Palakkad is a professional organization of engineering students, which aims at dedicating to advancing technology for the benefit of humanity. Since its establishment, IEEE SB NSSCE has actively conducted various events which helps students to mould their technical skills, innovation and creativity.

ABOUT

IEEE Special Interest Group
Humanitarian Technology [SIGHT]

IEEE Special interest group on humanitarian technology (SIGHT) is an affinity group that leverages technology for sustainable development. It aims at applying technical knowledge and skills of engineers to perform humanitarian activities which in turn aims at development of society and common people.

SHRUTHI

Shruthi is an initiative undertaken by IEEE SIGHT AG, NSSCE which aims at promoting the sustainable development goal of education by virtue of launch of educational audio textbooks for students who are specialy abled. In its first venture, this humanitarian activity which primarily focuses on students with visual and reading disabilities with an objective to promote equity and help them gain knowledge compared to their peers.
